Chat Bot with database

I developed a chatbot utilizing the ChatGPT API, which allows users to engage in natural language conversations. The chatbot is seamlessly integrated with a database to store chat history, enabling users to revisit previous interactions. The chatbot can provide informative responses while keeping track of user inputs and bot replies, creating a cohesive conversational flow.

Key Insights:

  • The age group of 50 to 60 has the highest distribution of billionaires.
  • Among the top 10 industries for billionaires aged 50 to 60, technology ranks as the number one industry.
  • The primary source of wealth for this age group is hedge funds.
  • Upon further analysis, the growth of billionaires within the age group of 50 to 60 showed that 1964 was the peak year, with 19 billionaires born in that year.
  • Most billionaires in this age group are self-made, indicating a high probability of self-made billionaires in this demographic.

Technologies Used:

  • Python: The primary programming language used for data analysis and visualization.
  • Pandas: Utilized for data manipulation, cleaning, and analysis.
  • Matplotlib: Used for creating static visualizations and charts.
  • Plotly Express: Used for creating interactive visualizations and in-depth data exploration.
