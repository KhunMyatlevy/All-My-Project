Self-Made vs. Inherited Wealth Analysis

In this project, I analyzed the distribution of self-made versus inherited wealth among billionaires. Using interactive charts, I visualized key differences in age, industries, and geographic locations. The analysis revealed notable trends, such as the dominance of self-made billionaires in technology and finance. This project provided a clear, data-driven view of wealth creation patterns across demographics.

Key Insights:

  • The United States has the highest number of billionaires according to the analysis of the top 10 countries by billionaire count.
  • In both country and industry analyses, most billionaires were found to be self-made.
  • Further analysis of the top 10 industries revealed that finance and investment had the highest number of billionaires born in 1960, with a total of 27.
  • The year 1960 also marked the peak year for billionaire births across all industries.

Technologies Used:

  • Python: The primary programming language used for data analysis and visualization.
  • Pandas: Utilized for data manipulation and analysis.
  • Plotly Express: Used for creating interactive visualizations and charts.
